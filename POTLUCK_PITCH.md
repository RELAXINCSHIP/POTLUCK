# 🎰 POTLUCK — Prize-Linked Savings

**What if saving money felt like winning the lottery?**

---

## The Problem

Americans don't save — but they love to gamble.

| Stat | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **56%** | Can't cover a $1,000 emergency |
| **$100B** | Spent on lottery tickets per year |
| **0.01%** | Average savings account APY |

Traditional savings accounts offer no excitement. People crave excitement, not spreadsheets — so they gamble away money they can't afford to lose.

---

## The Solution

**Potluck: Save money. Win prizes. Lose nothing.**

Users deposit money into FDIC-insured accounts. Instead of earning traditional interest, their yield goes into a shared prize pool. Every 90 days, someone wins big. Your principal? Always 100% safe and withdrawable.

---

## How It Works

```
🏦 Deposit          📈 Yield → Pool       🎰 Draw            🏆 Win
FDIC-insured    →   Interest funds    →   Quarterly +    →   Life-changing
savings              prizes               monthly            prizes
```

**Key Mechanics:**
- **Streak Multipliers** — The longer you stay, the more entries you earn (up to 3×)
- **Syndicates** — Pool entries with friends to boost collective odds
- **Near-Miss Feedback** — See your ranking after every draw; unlock bonus entries
